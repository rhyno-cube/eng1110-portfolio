MWA 1

The Power I Struggled to Concede

A reflection on sensitivity as a hidden strength.

For most of my life, I believed that being sensitive was a weakness. I remember sitting in my room after a long day...

But suppressing emotions doesn't make them disappear. It merely buried them. The turning point came when I began working closely with children...

"Instead of telling them to calm down, I told them that it was okay to feel upset."

I realized that the strength I was looking for was always there, concealed within the very softness I had previously attempted to eradicate.

Critical Reflection

In this project, I used critical thinking to bridge the gap between my personal feelings and my professional work with children. By analyzing my past misconception that sensitivity was a "flaw," I was able to reconstruct my narrative to show how interpersonal connection is actually a form of biological and emotional consciousness.

Design & Integration (CDIG)

  • Structural Layout: Utilized a grid system to separate the narrative, the reflection, and the technical metadata.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Implemented a stylized blockquote for the memoir’s climax to guide the reader's eye toward the shift in tone.
  • Digital Composition: Integrated custom Tailwind styling to ensure the portfolio remains professional yet accessible for peer review.
